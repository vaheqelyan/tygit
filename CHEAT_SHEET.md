### Track file

> <pre>
>  Windows/Linux <kbd>Ctrl</kbd> + <kbd>A</kbd>
>  OSX <kbd>⌘</kbd> + <kbd>A</kbd>
> </pre>
>
> Track all unstaged files and folders

> <pre>
>  Windows/Linux <kbd>L</kbd>
>  OSX <kbd>L</kbd>
> </pre>

Just press <kbd>i</kbd> in the list to ignore the selected file
